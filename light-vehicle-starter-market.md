<p><strong>The Light Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle starter market is categorized into two primary types: direct control and electromagnetic control. Direct control starters utilize a simple and direct mechanism for engaging the starter motor, often providing reliability and quick response. In contrast, electromagnetic control starters rely on electromagnetic forces to engage the starter, offering a more complex mechanism that can improve efficiency and reduce power consumption. Both types serve to initiate engine ignition but differ in their operational mechanisms and energy management.</p></p>
