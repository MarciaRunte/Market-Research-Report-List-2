<p><ul><li>Addition Type</li><li>Condensation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Two-Component RTV (Room Temperature Vulcanizing) rubber market is divided into two main types: addition type and condensation type. </p><p>Addition type RTV rubber cures through a reaction that adds silicon chains without the release of byproducts, offering superior properties and faster curing times. Conversely, condensation type RTV rubber cures by releasing small molecules like alcohol or water, resulting in a slower curing process and often yielding a more flexible end product. Both types play vital roles in various industrial applications, including automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
