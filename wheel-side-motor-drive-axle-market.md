<p><p>The Wheel-Side Motor Drive Axle market is categorized into two primary types: integrated and distributed systems. In contrast, distributed systems involve separate motor units, typically mounted closer to the vehicle's chassis, allowing for easier maintenance and flexibility in design. Both configurations aim to improve vehicle efficiency, handling, and acceleration while facilitating advancements in electric and autonomous vehicle technologies.</p></p>
