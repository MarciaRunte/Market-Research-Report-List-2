<p><p>Paper forming fabrics are a type of textile used in the paper manufacturing process, specifically during the wet phase of papermaking. <p><p>The paper forming fabrics market is divided into two main types: single layer forming fabrics and multi-layer forming fabrics. Single layer forming fabrics consist of a single layer of fibers, providing basic functionality for paper formation with simpler design and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, multi-layer forming fabrics feature several interconnected layers, enhancing drainage, sheet formation, and durability, making them suitable for more demanding paper production processes. Each type caters to different performance requirements and paper qualities in the industry.</p></p>
