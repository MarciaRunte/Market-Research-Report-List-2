<p><p>The machine vision spot light market is divided into two main types: adjustable focus type and non-focusable type. Adjustable focus type lights allow users to customize the beam's intensity and width, providing flexibility for various applications and environments. This adaptability makes them suitable for tasks requiring precision and control. Conversely, non-focusable type lights emit a fixed beam, offering uniform illumination without adjustments. These are ideal for standardized applications where consistent lighting is essential, ensuring even exposure across the inspection area.</p></p>
