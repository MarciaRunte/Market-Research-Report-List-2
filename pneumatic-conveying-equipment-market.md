<p><strong>The Pneumatic Conveying Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure Conveying</li><li>Vacuum Pressure Conveying</li><li>Combined Conveying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic conveying equipment market comprises three main types: positive pressure conveying, vacuum pressure conveying, and combined conveying. </p><p>Positive pressure conveying uses air or gas to push materials through a pipeline, ideal for short distances and lightweight materials. Vacuum pressure conveying relies on suction to draw materials into a conveying system, suitable for fragile or fine powders. Combined conveying integrates both methods, optimizing efficiency for diverse applications, enabling transportation over longer distances while accommodating various material types.</p></p>
