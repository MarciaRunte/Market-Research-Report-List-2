<p><p>Desiccant rotors are classified into three main market types: silica gel, molecular sieve, and others. Silica gel is widely used for its high absorption capacity and cost-effectiveness, making it suitable for various industrial applications. Molecular sieves offer precise moisture control due to their uniform pore structure, ideal for sensitive processes. Other desiccants may include zeolites and activated alumina, each serving niche markets based on specific moisture control needs, temperature ranges, and chemical compatibility. These types cater to diverse industrial demands.</p></p>
