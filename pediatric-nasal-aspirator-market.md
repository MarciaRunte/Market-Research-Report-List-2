<p><p>The pediatric nasal aspirator market is divided into two main types: electric nasal aspirators and manual nasal aspirators. Electric nasal aspirators utilize powered suction mechanisms, making them efficient for clearing nasal congestion with minimal effort from caregivers. They often come with adjustable suction levels and are designed for ease of use. In contrast, manual nasal aspirators operate through hand-operated suction, providing a more tactile approach. They are generally more affordable and portable but require more exertion from the user. Both types serve the essential purpose of alleviating nasal congestion in children.</p></p>
