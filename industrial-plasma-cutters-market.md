<p><p>The industrial plasma cutters market is primarily divided into two types: manual and CNC (Computer Numerical Control). Manual plasma cutters are operated by hand, allowing flexibility for intricate cutting tasks, often favored by smaller workshops or for on-site repairs. In contrast, CNC plasma cutters are automated, leveraging computer programming for precision and efficiency in high-volume production environments. This automation reduces human error, enhances repeatability, and is ideal for complex designs, making it popular in large-scale manufacturing industries.</p></p>
