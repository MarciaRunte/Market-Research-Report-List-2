<p><p>The meat starter culture market is segmented into two main types: maturation starters and surface starters. Maturation starters are used during the fermentation and aging process of various meat products, enhancing flavor and texture while controlling microbial activity. Surface starters, on the other hand, are applied to the outer layer of meat products, promoting desirable mold growth and protecting against spoilage bacteria. Both types play a crucial role in the development of safe, high-quality meat products.</p></p>
