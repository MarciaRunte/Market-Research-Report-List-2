<p><p>CO combustors are categorized into three main market types: Can, Cannular, and Annular. Can combustors are distinct units, typically used in smaller turbine engines, allowing for easier maintenance. Cannular combustors feature a combination of can and annular designs, offering enhanced efficiency and lower emissions. Annular combustors, designed as a continuous ring, provide better mixing and combustion efficiency, ideal for larger engines. Each type caters to specific applications, performance needs, and operational efficiency in various engine designs.</p></p>
