<p><p>The artificial heart-lung machine market features two primary types of pumps: single roller pumps and double roller pumps. Single roller pumps utilize a single roller mechanism to circulate blood, offering simplicity and efficiency, making them suitable for various clinical applications. In contrast, double roller pumps employ two rollers to provide enhanced blood flow control and reduce hemolysis, which is crucial during complex procedures. Both types cater to specific patient needs, impacting their adoption in hospitals and surgical centers.</p></p>
