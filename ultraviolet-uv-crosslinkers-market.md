<p><p>The Ultraviolet (UV) Crosslinkers market is categorized into three key types based on wavelength: 254nm shortwave UV, 302nm mediumwave UV, and 365nm longwave UV. Shortwave UV is effective for rapid curing processes, commonly used in sterilization and adhesive applications. Mediumwave UV offers a balance between speed and penetration, suitable for various coatings and inks. Longwave UV is primarily utilized for curing thick films and specific applications where deeper penetration is crucial, enhancing versatility across industrial uses.</p></p>
