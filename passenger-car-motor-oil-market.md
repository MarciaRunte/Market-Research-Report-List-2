<p><p>The passenger car motor oil market is primarily divided into two types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from refining crude oil and contains natural additives, offering basic protection and performance at a lower cost. Synthetic oil, on the other hand, is engineered chemically to provide superior performance, better temperature stability, and enhanced protection against wear and tear. Consumers often choose synthetic oil for higher mileage vehicles, while mineral oil remains popular for cost-sensitive users.</p></p>
