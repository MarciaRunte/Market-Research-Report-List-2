<p><ul><li>Balanced Coil Systems</li><li>Ferrous-in-Foil Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal detectors in the food market are essential for ensuring product safety and quality by identifying metallic contaminants. Balanced coil systems utilize a network of coils to create a balanced electromagnetic field, effectively detecting ferrous and non-ferrous metals in various products. Ferrous-in-foil systems, on the other hand, focus specifically on detecting ferrous metals within packaging materials, ensuring that even small metal fragments are identified. Both systems play a crucial role in maintaining food safety and preventing contamination in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
