<p><p>The Tuberculosis (TB) treatment drugs market is divided into two primary types: Active TB and Latent TB. Active TB requires intensive treatment regimens with multiple antibiotics to combat the live bacteria, as it poses a significant health risk and is contagious. In contrast, Latent TB involves individuals who have been infected but do not show symptoms or pose a transmission risk; treatment is aimed at preventing progression to active disease, typically using fewer drugs for a shorter duration.</p></p>
