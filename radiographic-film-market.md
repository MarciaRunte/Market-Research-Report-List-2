<p><p>The radiographic film market comprises several types of screens that enhance image quality. Lead screens provide efficient radiation shielding, allowing more controlled exposure and reducing patient dose. Fluorescent screens utilize special materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, improving image clarity and reducing exposure time. Fluorometallic screens combine the benefits of fluorescent and metallic properties, offering high sensitivity and durability. Together, these screens play a crucial role in optimizing radiographic imaging across various medical and industrial applications.</p></p>
