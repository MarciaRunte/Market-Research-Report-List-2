<p><p>The refrigerant lubricant market is primarily categorized into three types: mineral oil, synthetic oil, and others. Mineral oil, derived from refining crude oil, offers good lubrication but can be less effective in extreme temperatures. Synthetic oil, engineered from chemical compounds, provides superior performance, stability, and a wider operating temperature range, making it suitable for modern refrigerants. The 'others' category includes bio-based oils and specialty lubricants tailored for specific applications, enhancing efficiency and environmental sustainability in refrigeration systems.</p></p>
